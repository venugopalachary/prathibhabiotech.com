Bio Fungicides

Tricho Rich

Composition

Trichoderma Viride.


Uses

Due to excess or low moisture levels in the soil root rot, blast, powdery mildew will be increased. To prevent them, Trichoderma Viridi is the only way. It is grown with cattle manure and applied when there is moisture in the soil and it will act as a protection shield to the root system by eliminating the fungus that caused the root rot, blast, and powdery mildew. It will increase the immunity in the plants.


Dosage

It should be grown in 500 kgs cattle manure with 10 kgs of Trichorich and should be used when there is moisture in one acre soil.



Flick

Uses

When Flick is sprayed on the crop which contains the Pseudomonas Fluorescens bacteria would enter into the plant through holes of the leaf and settles down by releasing poisonous Pseudomone substance. Due to this the bacteria that causes leaf spot, stem rot, fruit rot, branch rot, blast, early blight will be destroyed. Thus plant will be protected from pests.


Dosage

Spray: Marinate 1 kg Flick in 1 kg jaggery for 24 hours and spray on an acre.


In soil

It should be grown in 500 kgs cattle manure with 10 kgs of Flick and should be used when there is moisture in one acre soil.

Vironil

Composition

Saponins: 80%. Fillers & Stabilizers: 20%, Total: 100% Mode of Action: Vironil enters in to the plant through the stomatal opening and is translocated in to the plant system through the vascular bundles. Vironil enters on the effected plant cell and encapsulates the virus particles. It reduces separation of virus particles in protein and nuclei particles in the absence of food the virus becomes in active. Vironil open up the conductive tissues blocked by the movement of virus particles and the plant cell starts to recover.


Targeted Disease

It controls the vectors of virus diesease. Spray preventively t o get good results.


Crops

Chilles, Cereals, Pulses & Oilseeds, Fruits and all vegetables crops.


Dosages

250g. Per 1 Acre. Repeat spray in an interval of 20 days for best results.
