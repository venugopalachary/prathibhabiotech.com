Zen-X

Composition

It contains micro minerals, amino acids, potassium, magnesium.


Uses

They are: Zinc (Zn), Manganese (Mn), Copper (Cu), Ferrous (Fe), Boron (B),Molybdenum (Mo) and Chlorine (CI). Use of these mixtures as per the deficiencies of micro minerals in the land will bring good results. By spraying Zen-X micro minerals deficiencies will be rectified and get higher yields.

Proteinex

Composition

Proteins , amino acids and micro minerals.


Uses

It contains Protein hydralicites and micro-minerals which are extracted from soyabean. It has natural proteins and amino acids. This will help to increase the natural growth of the plant. It has brasinolytes which is extracted from mustard flower (mustard seed) which is not only powerful but also helps in increasing the plants growth. It not only promotes more flowering, but it also aids the development of a big percentage of the flowering into a healthy fruit. It also keeps flowers from drooping/falling and gives pods and fruits a pleasant taste, size, and color. As a result, improved quality and yields are achieved.


Dosage

per one acre of crop use 300 gms.

Promo

Composition

Ammonia Molybdate 52%


Uses

We can rectify molybdenum (MO) deficiency in plants by using Promo in every plant. Nitrogen will be absorbed in the form of nitrates. Molybdenum only changes these into nitrates i.e as amino acids. That means molybdenum must be present in plants in order to fully utilize the nitrogen we use. It makes phosphorus in a natural form and brings into plants usage. This results in a natural growth of the plant and abundant flowering.


Dosage

per one acre of crop use 50 to 100 gms.
