Organic Fertilizers

Avani

Composition

It contains millettia pinnata, castor powder, neem powder and powerful bacteria.


Uses

This is a collection of organic and bio fertilizer. It is made with unique EM2 technology. Due to the bio regulators, organic carbon, bio fertilizers soil will become porous and will form a powerful root system and plant will grow healthy. The micro organisms in the root system of the plant act as a protection shield and will increase the immunity in the plant. This will let the waste in the soil to decompose, and the nitrogen, phosphorous, and potassium that are laying waste in the soil will be melted and sent to the plant.


Dosage

Per one acre crop use 40-80 kilos.



Pudami Ultra

Composition

Super Potassium Humate.


Uses

It contains Humic, Fulvic and potassium which is extracted from the mineral leonardite. This is most powerful organic carbon. It will transform into a usable stage very quickly and will be used as food for beneficial microbes which are in the soil and that are beneficial to the plants. Thus land will become porous, robust root system will be formed and will bring the chemical fertilizers that are lying wasted in the soil into use. It will enhance the soil to absorb the more water and makes the soil moisture content to stay for a long time. Hence it will help in higher yield.


Dosage

90-120 days crop = 3 times 20 Kg+10 Kg+10 Kg. 150-180 days crop= 6 times 20 Kg+10 Kg + 10 Kg + 10 Kg + 10 Kg + 10 Kg.


Bharath Gold Plus
